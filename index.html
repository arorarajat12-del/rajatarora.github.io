About

How I work

Short version

I work at the intersection of revenue operations, CRM governance, and systems enablement. I focus on converting strategy into operating cadence: clear funnel definitions (MQL/PQL/SQL), reliable owner routing, and workflows teams actually follow.

I’m strongest in cross-functional environments where sales, marketing, and operations are misaligned and need a practical operating model that scales.

What I value

  • Clarity over complexity
  • Governance over heroics
  • Systems that outlive people
  • Simple UX for operators
How do you usually start with a new team?

I start with lifecycle mapping + ownership clarity, then layer governance, then automate high-friction flows tied to KPI outcomes.

What’s your strongest differentiator?

I combine systems architecture depth with operator-first UX, so what gets built is both durable and adopted quickly.

Target fit

Where this operating model fits best

High-growth SaaS environments

Best aligned to environments similar to BioRender, AfterShip, AgencyAnalytics, MapsPeople, and EvenUp — where RevOps must balance speed, governance, and cross-functional accountability.

  • Demand-to-revenue ownership across Marketing, Sales, and Customer Success
  • PQL + lifecycle qualification models for PLG and hybrid funnels

2026 RevOps metric framework

  • CAC payback: target 6–12 months
  • LTV:CAC: maintain 3:1+ with visibility to 5:1 segments
  • Forecast accuracy: committed quarter variance within ~5%
  • NRR: support 120%+ through cleaner expansion signals
  • Lead response: enforce rapid routing to avoid 1-hour decay risk

Focus is not vanity reporting; it is volume, value, velocity, and conversion quality across the full lifecycle.
